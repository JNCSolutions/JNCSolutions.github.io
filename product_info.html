Construction Manager 50 is a unique solution for the Construction Industry to provide total and easy control of both contract management and small maintenance works.
Developed specifically for the Construction Industry and integrated with the market leading accounts system Sage 50 Accounts, Construction Manager will provide real benefits to companies who need to:

  • Know at the touch of a button which jobs are profitable and more important which are not!
  • Improve Cash Flow.
  • Greatly reduce the risk of expensive errors.
  • Integrate Job Costing directly to the accounts system.
  • Control Oustanding Applications and Retentions.
  • Simplify CIS
  • Reduce Administration costs
  • Mobilise the workforce with Integrated hand held devices PDA's

Construction Manager has been developed to meet the needs of builders, contractors and subcontrators in the industry and by utilising the new generation N-Tier technology the system is extremely fast and easily web enabled, thus future proving the investment. The software manages all aspects of the Construction Industry Business Practices.

  • Manages Tenders, Retention's, Applications and Certificates
  • Keeps track of Materials, Labour, Subcontractors and Plant. Dayworks, Time Sheets and Variations
  • Links seamlessly with Sage.
  • Tracks payments and CIS
  • Manages maintenance and repair work
  • Integrates with PDA's

Construction Manager 50 fills a gap in the market by adding an industry specific solution to the market leader in Accounting Solutions to meet all the requirements of the construction industry. Until now construction companies have had to choose between expensive industry solutions often with limited accounting capability or a standard accounts product not ideally suited to the Industry business practices. The combined capabilities of Sage Line 50 and Construction Manager 50 provide the best of class in each area with seamless integration and at a lower cost.

Compare Sage Construct with Construction Manager

The two main Construction Additions for Sage are Sage Construct and Construction Manager. Listed below is a comparison of the two.

Construction Manager has been designed to work specifically for Sage Line 50 and has a Line 50 format user interface and structure which makes it easier to use and quicker to set up than Sage Construct.

Sage Construct was originally designed for the Sage 100/200 range. Sage Construct works with Sage Line 50 but the style of the interface is very different and the operation and set up is more complex.

Both systems offer similar set of features but Construction Manager provides a solution that is cheaper to buy and run and easier to use. Why not arrange a demonstration and see for yourself.
