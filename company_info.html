About JNC Solutions Ltd


JNC has almost 20 years of experience developing software for construction in the UK. With some of the best software developers in the business JNC has developed innovative solutions to meet specific business needs in a cost effective way.

Following on from the success of CISManager50, Construction Manager 50 is the result of significant software investment to meet requirements from customers in the construction industry. New products which have beend added to the solution suite include Maintenance Manager 50 and PDA Manager 50.
