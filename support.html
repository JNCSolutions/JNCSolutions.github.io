Support - Construction Software

Customer support is a vital ingredient in ensuring efficient usage of key business software. Because JNC specialises in the Constuction Industry our support team are very knowledgeable about the industry requirements and application of our software.

JNC Solutions Ltd provides a comprehensive support service which includes :

Telephone Support - Direct call to help desk or email response

On Line Support - Our service includes remote access via our web site so we can see your screen and help with advice and technical issues on line.

Software updates - Are included with support and can be downloaded from our web site.

On Site Support - is available at additional cost for installation, set up, training and consultancy.
